About me

Hello, my name is Javid. After working as a music teacher for more than 10 years, I decided to become a programmer. The reason for this is that this art is both interesting and does not depend on any place. After studying in guitar and piano classes in the field of music, I continued my art by teaching both privately and in public schools. I received my higher education at the Azerbaijan State University of Culture and Arts at the bachelor's, master's and doctoral levels. My higher education specialty was International Cultural Relations. However, I have not worked anywhere in this specialty. The reason for this is that this type of work takes up all weekdays and weekends, which is an obstacle to personal development. We can consider my higher education as a mistake of youth)) I can introduce myself as a "self-taught" student in the field of programming. I started taking Udemy classes in C and C++ first after taking the advice of several experts. Later on, I learned HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, REACT, NODE, EXPRESS JS, MongoDB, Mongoose, Tailwind and Material UI.
